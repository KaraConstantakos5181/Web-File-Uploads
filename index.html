Here are some fun facts about horses... that everyone should know :-)

  1. A few things that a horse eats is grass, hay (dried grass), grain, pepper mints, carrots, apples.
  2. A girl horse is referred to as a Mare.
  3. A boy horse is referred to as a Gelding or Stallion.
  4. Horses range in color from black to spotted(paint).
  5. Horses are natuarlly pack animals and like to be in groups whether they are domesticated or not.
  6. Horses are different from poines! In fact, a pony is smaller than a horse even when it is full grown. Many people often confuse poines with baby horses (a foal).
